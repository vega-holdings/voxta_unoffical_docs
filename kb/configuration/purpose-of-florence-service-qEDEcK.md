# Purpose of Florence Service

> Florence enables local GPU-based computer vision capabilities.

## Answer

Florence is an advanced vision foundation model designed to run computer vision tasks locally on your GPU. It handles vision-language tasks through a prompt-based approach, allowing the AI to 'see' and interpret images.
