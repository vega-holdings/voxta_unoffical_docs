# Troubleshooting Flashcap Video Capture Stability

> Fix Flashcap stability by manually selecting the device instead of using Auto and restarting the server.

## Answer

To improve stability with Flashcap video capture:

1. Change the device selection from **Auto** to **Primary** or manually select the specific webcam/device instead of leaving it on Auto.
2. Ensure you click **Save**.
3. Restart the Voxta server to apply changes.
