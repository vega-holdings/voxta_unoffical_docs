# Retrieving Application Logs

> Instructions for downloading logs via the F2 Diagnostics menu.

## Answer

To retrieve logs for troubleshooting:
1. Reproduce the issue you are experiencing.
2. Press `F2` to open the Diagnostics/Dashboard.
3. Click the **Download Log** button.
