# Fix 'Forbidden' errors when loading voices or models

> Resolve 'Forbidden' errors by linking Patreon and Discord accounts.

## Answer

This error typically indicates that the Voxta server cannot verify your subscription status. To resolve this, ensure your Patreon account is connected to your Discord account.
