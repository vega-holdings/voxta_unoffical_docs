# Resolving VAM Scene Loading Warnings

> Clear VAM cache to resolve scene loading warnings.

## Answer

Try clearing your Virt-A-Mate (VAM) cache. Caching is often enabled by default for performance reasons, but it can sometimes cause assets to fail to load correctly or generate warnings.
