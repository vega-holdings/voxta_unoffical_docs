# Recommended Stable Diffusion Checkpoints and LoRAs

> List of recommended Stable Diffusion checkpoints and LoRAs for image generation.

## Answer

The following Stable Diffusion checkpoints and LoRAs are recommended for image generation setups:

* **Balanced (Digital Art/Realism):** [Civitai Model](https://civitai.com/models/1110783?modelVersionId=2110275)
  * Recommended LoRA: [Incase Style PonyXL](https://civitai.com/models/300005/incase-style-ponyxl) (suggested at half strength).
* **Anime Style (Tutorial Standard):** [Civitai Model](https://civitai.com/models/827184?modelVersionId=1367422) (V10.0, adaptable).
* **Fast/Realistic:** [Civitai Model](https://civitai.com/models/1412827?modelVersionId=1643845)
  * Recommended Sliders: [Ass Size Slider](https://civitai.com/models/477074/ass-size-slider-pony-illustrious) and [Illustrious Realism Slider](https://civitai.com/models/1486904/illustrious-realism-slider).
