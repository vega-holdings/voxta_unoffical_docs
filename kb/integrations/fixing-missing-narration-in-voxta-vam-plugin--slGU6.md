# Fixing Missing Narration in Voxta VAM Plugin

> Assign the narrator in the Voxta VAM plugin settings to enable narration audio.

## Answer

To enable narration within the Voxta VAM (Virt-A-Mate) plugin:

1. Open the Voxta VAM plugin interface.
2. Scroll down to locate the **Narrator** slot.
3. Manually assign the same narrator (voice/character) that is used in your Voxta scenario to this slot.
